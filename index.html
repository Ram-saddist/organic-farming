Welcome to my Website

OrgFarm 360 is a comprehensive website that serves as a valuable resource for individuals interested in starting organic farming in India. The website provides a wealth of information on various aspects of organic farming, including its key characteristics, advantages, and profitability in the Indian context. It offers practical guidance on how to initiate organic farming, covering topics such as soil preparation, nutrient management, weed and pest control, and disease management. Additionally, the website highlights the government schemes and funding options available to support organic farming ventures, including information on bank loans and investment requirements. By addressing common questions and concerns, OrgFarm 360 aims to equip aspiring organic farmers with the knowledge and resources they need to successfully embark on this sustainable and profitable agricultural journey.
